\# Clinical Example



\## User Input



目标期刊：Example Clinical Journal



Aims/scope：关注临床风险预测、诊断和患者管理。



研究主题：Biomarker Z predicts adverse outcomes in patients with condition Q.



主要结果：



\- Higher Z is associated with adverse outcomes.

\- Z improves risk stratification in multivariable models.

\- Study is retrospective and single-center.



\## Suggested Diagnosis



当前故事可定位为：



> Biomarker Z may improve risk stratification for patients with condition Q.



\## Recommended Strategy



Translational-impact narrative with conservative claim calibration.



\## Polishing Logic



1\. Introduction 从 condition Q 的风险分层需求切入。

2\. 总结现有预测方法不足。

3\. 指出 Z 的潜在价值尚未充分评估。

4\. Discussion 中强调 association，而非 causal effect。

5\. Limitation 中明确 retrospective single-center design。



\## Claim Calibration



Avoid:



> Z is a reliable clinical predictor ready for routine use.



Use:



> Z may serve as a candidate marker for risk stratification, pending validation in larger prospective cohorts.
