# Social Science Example

## User Input

目标期刊：Example Social Science Journal

Aims/scope：关注社会行为、制度影响和理论发展。

研究主题：Digital work platforms and worker autonomy.

主要结果：

- Platform rules increase flexibility in some contexts.
- Algorithmic monitoring reduces perceived autonomy.
- Effects differ by worker experience.

## Suggested Diagnosis

当前故事可定位为：

> Digital platforms create a conditional form of autonomy shaped by algorithmic governance and worker experience.

## Recommended Strategy

Theory-building + controversy-resolution narrative.

## Polishing Logic

1. Introduction 从关于平台劳动自主性的争议切入。
2. 说明已有研究存在二分化理解：flexibility vs control。
3. 提出本文考察情境条件和调节因素。
4. Discussion 中强调理论贡献：autonomy is conditional, not absolute.
5. 避免将特定样本结果推广到所有平台劳动者。

## Claim Calibration

Avoid:

> Digital platforms reduce worker autonomy.

Use:

> Digital platforms may reshape worker autonomy in ways that depend on governance mechanisms and worker experience.
