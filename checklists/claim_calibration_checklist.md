# Claim Calibration Checklist

## 1. Evidence Type

判断每个核心 claim 的证据类型：

- Direct experimental evidence
- Statistical association
- Observational correlation
- Exploratory finding
- Hypothesis or speculation
- Literature-based interpretation

## 2. Verb Selection

### Strong Evidence

可用：

- demonstrate
- show
- reveal
- establish

慎用，需直接证据支持。

### Moderate Evidence

推荐：

- suggest
- indicate
- provide evidence that
- support the possibility that
- are consistent with

### Weak Evidence

推荐：

- may
- might
- could
- raise the possibility that
- warrant further investigation

## 3. Red Flags

检查是否存在：

- 把相关性写成因果性
- 把初步发现写成确定结论
- 把体外结果直接推广到临床
- 把单中心研究推广到所有人群
- 使用 first/novel/unique 但无文献支持
- 使用 prove 但证据不足

## 4. Safer Rewrites

Instead of:

> X causes Y.

Use:

> X may contribute to Y.

Instead of:

> This proves that...

Use:

> These findings provide evidence that...

Instead of:

> This method can be used clinically.

Use:

> This method may have potential for future clinical evaluation.
